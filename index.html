About This Site

Hi there! Welcome to my personal website, where I share my journey through bioinformatics, biostatistics, and data analysis. This is a space for me to document my learning, explore new tools, and reflect on the challenges and joys of working in this field.

What You’ll Find Here

  • Tool Tutorials: My experiences with bioinformatics tools, including tips, tricks, and troubleshooting.
  • Paper Summaries: Reflections on papers I’ve read, focusing on key takeaways and how they relate to my work or interests.
  • Thoughts on Learning: Musings on the challenges of being a PhD candidate, the joy of discovery, and the never-ending process of learning.
